FAQ

Stainless hinge questions, answered

Will 304 rust outdoors?

Inland and rinsed by rain, 304 holds up for years. Within a few miles of saltwater or in de-icing-salt zones, it will tea-stain and eventually pit — move to 316.

Is 316 worth the premium for an interior door?

Rarely. For dry interior use 304 is fully adequate. Save 316 for chloride or chemical exposure where it actually earns its cost.

Are either grade magnetic?

Both are austenitic and essentially non-magnetic when annealed. Heavy cold-working in the knuckle can induce slight magnetism — normal and harmless.
