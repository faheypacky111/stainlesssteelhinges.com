How it decides

Material sets the mounting style (strap and T-hinges for wood, weld-on or bolt-on barrel hinges for metal, broad bolt-on plates for vinyl). Weight sets the size and whether you need a third hinge. Coastal exposure forces 316. And a pool gate triggers a self-closing, self-latching recommendation, which is a code requirement in most areas.

Frequently asked questions

Why does a pool gate change the answer?

Pool barriers almost always must self-close and self-latch by code, and the chlorine environment calls for 316. Confirm your local code.

How many hinges for a heavy gate?

Three, to prevent sag and twist. Light gates can use two.
